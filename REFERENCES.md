# References / <span lang="fr">Références</span>

This file catalogues the policy instruments, statutes, standards, and specification that the seven `gc-review-*` skills cite as the correct or successor anchor for a skill's rules. Verification status is recorded as of **2026-07-10**. Items that could not be confirmed against an authoritative source are explicitly marked **UNVERIFIED**. The orchestrator skill (`gc-review-all`) makes no standalone policy claims and defers to the domain skills below.

Ce fichier répertorie chaque instrument de politique, loi, norme et spécification que citent les sept compétences `gc-review-*` identifié comme l'ancrage correct ou successeur des règles d'une compétence. ## Succession notes / <span lang="fr">Notes de succession</span>

1. **Web accessibility.** The TBS *Standard on Web Accessibility* and *Guideline on Making Information Technology Usable by All* were rescinded **2026-03-02** by the GC CIO's *Direction on ICT Accessibility; towards regulatory implementation*. There is **no successor TBS instrument** — the 2022 draft *Standard on ICT Accessibility* (SICTA) was never brought into force. The binding successor is regulatory: **SOR/2025-255** (Digital Technologies Accessibility Regulations, registered 2025-12-05) incorporates **CAN/ASC - EN 301 549:2024** (which requires WCAG **2.1** AA, not 2.2) by reference — mandatory for GC web pages from **2027-12-05** and mobile apps/non-web documents from 2028-12-05, with accessibility statements, conformance assessments, and triennial training obligations. Interim regime: no regression below the former WCAG 2.0 baseline; CAN/ASC - EN 301 549:2024 strongly encouraged now. A WCAG 2.2 update to CAN/ASC is expected in 2026.
2. **Metadata.** The *Standard on Metadata* (2010-07-01) was rescinded **2024-01-24** and replaced by **Appendix L: Standard for Managing Metadata** of the Directive on Service and Digital (effective 2024-01-15 — process requirements only; it defines no field set). The current mandatory concept baseline is LAC's *Operational Standard for Digital Archival Records' Metadata* (effective **2023-11-16**, 12 system-agnostic mandatory concepts), made binding for DSD-s.6 departments via the TBS *Metadata Reference Standard for Digital Archival Records* (effective **2025-06-23**, under DSD s. 4.3.1). The "Common Core" label traces to IMCC, a non-public GCDOCS/EDRMS configuration profile — not a policy mandate.
3. **GC sign-in services.** **Sign In Canada** has been maintenance-only since **2023-07-31** (no new applications; new customers directed to SSC's **GC Credential Federation (GCCF)**, broker paths `fjgc-gccf.gc.ca`). The successor is **CanadaLogin** (login.canada.ca), launched 2025-02-12 by CDS/ESDC, carrying forward GCKey/Interac sign-ins. The Sign-In Canada brand's formal retirement date and the CIAM contract award details remain **UNVERIFIED**. Note: no GC instrument designates an approved-IdP list; the binding hook is the Directive on Identity Management s. 4.1.9.
4. **Communications and federal identity.** The 2016 communications policy suite (Policy on Communications and Federal Identity, 2016-05-11; *Directive on the Management of Communications*, archived 2025-03-27) was replaced on **2025-03-27** by the updated *Policy on Communications and Federal Identity* and the *Directive on the Management of Communications and Federal Identity* (requirement 4.1.13 effective 2026-03-27).
5. **Official languages on the web.** The *Directive on the Use of Official Languages on Web Sites* and the *Directive on the Use of Official Languages in Electronic Communications* were replaced on **2012-11-19** by the *Directive on Official Languages for Communications and Services* (s. 6.6.3.2 on website addresses took effect 2013-07-31).

<div lang="fr">

1. **Accessibilité Web.** La *Norme sur l'accessibilité des sites Web* du SCT a été abrogée le 2026-03-02 par la Direction du DPI sur l'accessibilité des TIC; le successeur contraignant est réglementaire : le **DORS/2025-255**, qui incorpore par renvoi la norme CAN/ASC - EN 301 549:2024 (pages Web du GC obligatoires à compter du 2027-12-05).
2. **Métadonnées.** La *Norme sur les métadonnées* (2010) a été abrogée le 2024-01-24 et remplacée par l'annexe L de la Directive sur les services et le numérique, complétée par la norme opérationnelle de BAC (2023-11-16) et la norme de référence du SCT (2025-06-23).

4. **Communications et image de marque.** La suite de politiques de 2016 a été remplacée le 2025-03-27 par la *Politique* et la *Directive sur la gestion des communications et de l'image de marque*.
5. **Langues officielles sur le Web.** Les directives sur l'usage des langues officielles dans les sites Web et les communications électroniques ont été remplacées le 2012-11-19 par la *Directive sur les langues officielles pour les communications et services*.
